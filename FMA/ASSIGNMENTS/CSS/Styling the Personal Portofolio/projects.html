My Projects

Project 1-How To Make Math Easier

This project is intended to make an app so that math students in the future have it easier to search online for information related to math. Basically you can learn math on this app without a teacher and you can solve problems by just taking a photo of the problem, then you're given the solution and the answer, this also works for word problems. It's very useful for math students that compete in math olympiads. It's a very difficult project, but I hope that one day I complete it!

Project 2-Making Studying Easier

In this project I plan on developing an app that can make studying easier in various different ways. One of the features is that you scan photos of textbooks, and notes and the app can summarise them in short points while keeping the main idea and maybe adding some necessary information. The app also works for online books and content, and it will also provide a short quiz for testing you and your knowledge. Teachers can also make exams and tests and submit them for their students to complete them before the deadline. You can also share videos and presentations with people and in this way you can build a community where people share their knowledge.

Project 3-Cooking Guide

For this project I will make an app that has thousands of cooking recipees from different cultures. You can also invent your own recipee and share it with others. The special thing about this app is that if you have no idea what to make with the ingredients that are available, you can type your ingredients and the app will give you a quick and easy recipee or a hard and complex one depending on your mood. You can also make videos and share them with other people so that in that way people can also learn how to cook.
