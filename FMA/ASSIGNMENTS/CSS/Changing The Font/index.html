What are fonts?

Fonts are a set of characters (letters, numbers, symbols, and punctuation marks) that share a consistent style and design. Essentially, a font is a specific design of text, with characteristics such as size, weight, slant, width, and other elements that give it its distinctive look.

What are they used for?

Fonts are used across various media, including print, digital design, websites, advertising, and more. Different fonts can evoke different emotions or impressions, which is why the choice of font is important in graphic design and branding.
