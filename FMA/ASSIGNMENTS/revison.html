What is Kosovo?

Kosovo is a country in south-eastern Europe. It is mainly dominated by the albanian ethnicity, and the official language is albanian. The official religion is islam.

Kosovo is really small and not very known.

Fun Facts

  1. Independence – Declared on February 17, 2008.
  2. Capital: Pristina
  3. Population – Around 1.8 million, mostly ethnic Albanians.
  4. Currency – Uses the Euro (€).
  5. Young Population – One of Europe’s youngest, 50% under 25.
